INTRODUCTION

Introduction: Coding Education in Government Schools is an initiative by Global Technopower Foundation (GTPF) aimed at bridging the educational gap and empowering young minds with digital skills. The program targets students in grades 8 to 10 from marginalized backgrounds attending under-resourced government schools.
